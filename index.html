Mixlet

A mini player for YouTube Music on macOS.

Small, focused, and a little better than living in a browser tab.

Download

via the Mac App Store

Why Mixlet?

It’s basically Safari Add to Dock—but a bit sharper and a lot more fun.

  • MINI: The mini player is the main feature, not an afterthought.
  • FAST: Media keys and keyboard shortcuts actually feel native.
  • RESUME: Picks up at the exact song and timestamp.
  • MAC‑LIKE: Clean, focused mac‑assed UI.

Full Web View, Too

  • PRIVATE: Runs in WebKit with macOS sandboxing, so Mixlet never sees your Google/YouTube credentials.
  • ALL‑FEATURES: Playlists, search, and sign‑in—just like YouTube Music on web and mobile.
