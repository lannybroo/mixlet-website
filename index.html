Mixlet

A mini player for YouTube Music on macOS.

A focused, lightweight way to keep music close without living in a browser tab.

Download

v1.0 for macOS 26

Why Mixlet?

  • Mini player is the primary UI, not an afterthought.
  • Better than Safari Add to Dock:
    • Full set of keyboard shortcuts and media key support.
    • Resume playback at the same song, same time stamp.
    • Clean, focused mac-assed UI.

Full Web View

  • Full view for your YouTube Music playlists, search, and login.
