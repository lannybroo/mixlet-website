Mixlet's Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 15, 2026

Mixlet is designed to be private by default. Mixlet wraps YouTube Music in a sandboxed macOS WebKit view and does not run its own analytics, ads, or tracking.

What Mixlet collects

Mixlet does not collect personal data, does not create user accounts, and does not sell data.

What stays on your Mac

Mixlet stores limited local app settings (for example window position and playback UI state) using standard macOS local storage mechanisms.

Google / YouTube Music

When you sign in to YouTube Music, authentication and account data are handled by Google inside WebKit. Mixlet does not have access to your password.

Network access

Mixlet connects to YouTube/Google services required for playback and sign-in. Mixlet does not operate a separate telemetry backend.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
